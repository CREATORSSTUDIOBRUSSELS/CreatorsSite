About Us

CREATORS STUDIO is a unique creative and collaborative space in Brussels, designed for artists, entrepreneurs, and multimedia enthusiasts. Our studio combines creativity, visual innovation and technology to bring your projects to life. We provide a complete working environment with professional equipment: equipped photo and video studios, lighting, various backdrops, editing stations and graphic design tools. More than just a studio, it's a true multimedia playground where imagination becomes reality.

Services & Equipment

Discover our professional equipment and services for your creative projects:

Studio Photo

Our studios offer a wide variety of colored backdrops to suit all visual styles: neutral backgrounds, bright colors, textures and original sets. The space is equipped with professional equipment: 5 flashes and 4 continuous lights with stands and different softboxes as well as decorative material to bring your ideas to life and enhance your productions.

Post-Production

Our team takes over after capture to enhance each project. We offer:

  • Photo editing: retouching, color grading, visual harmonization
  • Video editing: pre-production, filming, editing, color grading
  • Designing: graphic creation and visual direction

Art Direction

Our expertise in art direction accompanies you in defining, harmonizing and expressing the visual aspect of your universe. Custom creative support to refine your image, strengthen your identity and give aesthetic coherence to each of your projects.

Coworking Space

Our workspaces, desks and private offices are available for rent with flexible packages, including or excluding access to the photo studio. An ideal environment for creative professionals looking for a stimulating and collaborative workspace.

Make Up Area

A dedicated space with a large mirror and optimal lighting for makeup preparation before your shoots. This area is designed to ensure comfort and efficiency during your preparation, with easy access to the studio.

Versatil Space

Our versatile space is designed to adapt to your needs: photo shoots, video recordings, workshops, meetings and creative events. With modular equipment and a flexible layout, this space can be transformed to host a wide variety of projects and activities.
